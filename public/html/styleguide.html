How to Produce Certain Styles in SpiderRock

Hello. Here you'll find some basics on how to produce certain styles in SpiderRock. This guide assumes the author has at least a basic understanding of HTML.

Note: Wordpress has a WYSIWYG editor that generates most of the basic code for you. The styleguide documents ore complex design elements that fall outside of the bounds of Wordpress.
